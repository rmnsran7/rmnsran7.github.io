Paste your Firebase web config or service account JSON. Your data stays in your browser.

How do I get my Firebase config?
1. Go to console.firebase.google.com → your project
2. Click the gear icon next to "Project Overview" → Project Settings
3. Scroll to "Your apps" — if no web app exists, click </> to create one
4. Copy the firebaseConfig object and paste it above
