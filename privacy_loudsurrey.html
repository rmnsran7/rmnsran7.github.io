Privacy Policy for LoudSurrey

Effective Date: October 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LoudSurrey ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use LoudSurrey (the "Service"). Our service is designed to allow you to upload images and videos directly to your Instagram account via the official Meta Graph API.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our core functionality, we need to access certain information. We only collect what is essential for the Service to operate.

a) Information You Provide Directly:

  • Media Content: The images, videos, and associated text (captions) that you select to upload to your Instagram account.

b) Information We Receive from Meta (Facebook/Instagram):

When you connect your Instagram account to our Service, we receive the following information via the Meta Graph API:

  • Access Token: A secure token that grants our Service permission to post content on your behalf. We store this token securely.
  • Instagram Business Account ID: A unique identifier for your Instagram account, which is required to direct the API to post to the correct account.
  • Facebook Page ID: The identifier for the Facebook Page linked to your Instagram Business Account.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, friend list, or any other profile information beyond the necessary IDs and permissions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the sole purpose of providing and maintaining the Service. Specifically:

  • To Publish Content: We use your Access Token, Instagram Business Account ID, and the media content you provide to publish posts to your Instagram account as you direct.
  • To Maintain Connection: The Access Token is stored securely to maintain the connection between our Service and your Instagram account, so you do not have to re-authenticate for every upload.
  • To Fulfill API Requirements: The information we collect is strictly what is required by the Meta Graph API for content publishing functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

Your privacy is paramount. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your information is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Meta (Facebook/Instagram): To perform the primary function of the Service, we must transmit your media content and Access Token to Meta's servers via the Graph API. This usage is governed by Meta's own Platform Terms and Data Policy.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or prevent fraud or abuse of the Service.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we handle from loss, theft, and unauthorized access.

  • Access Tokens are treated as highly sensitive data and are stored in an encrypted and secure manner on our servers.
  • Media Content (your images, videos, and captions) is handled transiently. We process it for the purpose of uploading it to Instagram and do not store it on our servers after the upload is successfully completed or has failed.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your Instagram Business Account ID and encrypted Access Token for as long as you keep your account connected to our Service. If you choose to disconnect our Service, this information is securely deleted from our systems.

6. Your Control and Rights

You have full control over the permissions you grant our Service. You can revoke our access to your Instagram account at any time by visiting your Instagram settings: Settings → Security → Apps and Websites. Revoking permission will immediately prevent our Service from being able to post to your account, and we will delete your stored credentials.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
